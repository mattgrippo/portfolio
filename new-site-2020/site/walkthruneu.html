Project Background

This project was developed for the course 5D Fundamentals at Northeastern. The class was heavily focused on psychogeography, or the exploration of urban environments that emphasizes playfulness.

The assignment was to create an experience for a user to follow within the surrounding area, something that would guide them to discover new things and to see familiar things in a new light. One day as I was walking through campus, I started to read a plaque that described the history of the building that my next class was in. It provided insight into the buildings identity and gave me new appreciation for those who had come before me.

This became the seed for my project idea, and I started to gather the necessary requirements to create the walking tour through the school's history. This was also the time that I started to learn HTML and CSS, and while it wasn't a part of the project to actually develop it, I decided to use my new skills to create the experience that I had imagined in my head (although rudimentary in design, it is functional!).
