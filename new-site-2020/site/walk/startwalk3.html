Speare Hall

Find the bronze plaque of Frank Speare (pictured above) on the leftside wall of vestibule.


Tap for directions to Speare Hall
Walk out the main entrance of the YMCA and cross Huntington Avenue towards Amelia's Tacqueria. Make an immediately left down Huntington and a right onto Opera Place. Then make a left on Speare Place and the front entrance of Speare Hall will be on your right.
