Top Spotify Songs by Year



Below is a chart of the top Spotify songs by year from 2010 to 2019 compared across beats per minute (BPM) and danceability. This data comes from Spotify's algorithms and determines how to serve music organically to the user. The colors represent the genres of each song.

This project was developed for Northeastern University's Visualization Technologies course in the spring of 2020. The dataset can be found at Kaggle.com.
